Top nature experiences in Thailand

Picturesque jungle hikes and bucket-list wildlife encounters can all be on the agenda when you plan a getaway to Thailand. Not just home to a spectacular coastline, Thailand’s interiors are also unmissable.

a unique experience

Khao Sok National Park is a dense area of protected rainforest in the Phanom District. Explore it on foot or traverse the Sok River in a canoe. Scaling Krabi’s limestone cliffs is a must for more adventurous visitors! The region is a rock climber’s paradise, with numerous schools offering guided trips to tackle its ancient karsts. Hawksbill turtles, leopard sharks and manta rays are just some of the things you might see on a diving expedition in the Andaman Sea. Stay on the Similan Islands for underwater adventures.

A major highlight of holidays to Thailand is seeing elephants. Riding these gentle giants may not be on your agenda, but you can spot them in the wild at Khao Yai National Park. Thailand is overflowing with secret beaches only accessible by boat. Venture to Trang Province to swim or kayak into Emerald Cave. Vibrant green water gives way to a cove rumoured to have once harboured pirate treasure.
